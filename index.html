Privacy Policy for MarginBase

Last updated: July 12, 2026

MarginBase ("the app") is a paid, offline business calculator. We designed it to collect nothing about you. This policy explains, plainly, what that means.

Data we collect

None. MarginBase does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. It has no user accounts, no sign-in, no cloud, and no servers. It does not contain advertising, analytics, crash reporting, or any third-party tracking SDK.

Data stored on your device

To make the app useful, MarginBase saves a small amount of data only on your device:

  • Your most recent inputs (up to the last 5 entries per calculator), so you can reuse them.
  • Your resolved app language/locale setting.

This data never leaves your device. It is stored in the app's private storage and is not transmitted anywhere. You can remove it at any time by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling the app.

Network use

MarginBase works fully offline and makes no network requests for its functionality. It does not send your inputs, results, or any usage information over the internet.

Payments

MarginBase is a one-time paid app. Your purchase is processed by Google Play, not by us. We do not receive, see, or store your payment details. Google Play's own privacy policy governs the purchase transaction.

Children

MarginBase is a general business utility intended for adults (small-business owners and solo operators). It is not directed to children and is not part of the Google Play Families program.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the date above and publish the new version at this URL.
